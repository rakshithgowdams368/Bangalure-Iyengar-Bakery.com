History

Baked goods have been around in 2019. The art of baking was developed early during the N.S Gate. It was highly famous art as Dhrmavaram citizens loved baked goods and demanded them frequently for important occasions such as feasts and weddings. Because of the fame of the art of baking, around 300 BC, baking was introduced as an occupation and respectable profession for India. Bakers began to prepare bread at home in an oven, using mills to grind grain into flour for their breads. The demand for baked goods persisted, and the first bakers' guild was established in 168 BC in India. The desire for baked goods promoted baking throughout Kings and expanded into eastern parts of Asia. Bakers started baking bread and other goods at home and selling them on the streets.
